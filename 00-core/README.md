## How to Use This Section

1. **Reference Your Mental OS**: When making major life/career decisions, review your Mental OS
2. **Apply Frameworks**: Use decision frameworks when facing complex choices
3. **Reflect on Principles**: Review life principles during personal development
4. **Use Templates**: Apply templates for weekly reviews and communications

## Best Practices

- Keep this section **focused and minimal** - only core systems
- **Update when values change** - review annually at minimum
- **Version important documents** - track evolution of your thinking
- **Archive outdated frameworks** - keep only what's actively useful
- **Review quarterly** - ensure frameworks still serve you

## Important Notes

- **This is not**: A daily notes folder, project storage, or task management system
- **This is**: Reference material for decision-making and personal development
- **Archival**: Move outdated frameworks to personal-knowledge-base/archived-notes/
- **Growth**: Add new frameworks as you develop better systems
